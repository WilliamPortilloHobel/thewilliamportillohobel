To Los Angeles,

Did you know that there is a current healthcare shortage and we are in need of assistance?

How did the healthcare shortage happen? As you know, the state of the Earth change in 2020 which coronavirus and many healthcare workers decided to resign to be with their loved ones.

When the healthcare workers that stayed, they recieved a major burnout that even lead to more resignations!

In order to counter this healthcare burnout, we have decided to give scholarships to new incoming students at selected colleges

Once done with your program, we will provide higher income and high-paying travel assignments across the country

These programs include Respiratory Care in Los Angeles Valley College, Antelope Valley College, Concorde Career College at North Hollywood. Nursing programs in UCLA, Marian College at Van Nuys, West Coast University, and Pacific College.
